Frequently Asked

Common Questions

What does SAYANJALI NEXUS PRIVATE LIMITED do?+

SAYANJALI NEXUS PRIVATE LIMITED is a Hyderabad-based technology company building AI products, an independent Layer-1 blockchain, enterprise SaaS, and open-source developer tools — founded and led by Syed Ali Hasan Moosavi.

What is SAYANJALI BLOCKCHAIN?+

SAYANJALI BLOCKCHAIN is an independent Layer-1 blockchain built from scratch in Python, with Proof-of-Work consensus, ECDSA wallets, signed transactions, a REST API, and a CLI. It is the native settlement layer for the SYJ Token, shipped as v0.1.0-mvp with 37 passing automated tests.

Who founded SAYANJALI NEXUS?+

SAYANJALI NEXUS PRIVATE LIMITED was founded by Syed Ali Hasan Moosavi, who serves as Founder and Managing Director, alongside three co-directors.

Which business verticals are currently live?+

Seven verticals are live today: Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain & Web3, Enterprise SaaS, Developer Tools & Open Source, Cybersecurity & OSINT, Corporate Compliance, and Education Technology. Agriculture, logistics, cloud kitchen, real estate, e-commerce, consulting, and community development are on the future roadmap.

How can I contact SAYANJALI NEXUS?+

You can reach the team via email at cto@sayanjalinexus.com, by WhatsApp, or through the contact form on this website. The company is based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India.
